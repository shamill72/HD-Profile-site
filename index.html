Built Employee Annual Review Web Application in Laravel/PHP

Cabinets To Go, Gracious Home, Southwind Building Products (2022)

Employee annual reviews were still done on paper when I arrived at Cabinets To Go. With over 400 employees, it proved challenging to expedite the review process for the executive and HR teams. I was handed a printout of the review form and was asked if I could make this into a digital format form. After many questions, I realized this was not going to be a small task. All data needed to be stored for each employee. There needed to be a heirarchical system so that store manager could see their employee reviews, but could not see any other employee review. Regional managers needed to see all the store managers reviews and their employee reviews, but not any other reviews outside of their region. There also needed to be an approval process that, once approved by lower tier managers, no more editing could be done to the review data. Finally, the review had to be exported to a pdf to be uploaded to the company DocuSign system. This has become an every changing and evolving project. I have been the sole programmer on this project. I used Laravel 9, PHP, MySQL, Vanilla Javascript, and JQuery to develop this project.
